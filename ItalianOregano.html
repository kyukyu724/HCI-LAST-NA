Italian Oregano

Italian Oregano is a Mediterranean herb adapted into Filipino cooking for its robust, slightly peppery flavor. Commonly used in pasta dishes, pizzas, and marinades, it is also grown at home for its culinary versatility and medicinal benefits, especially in treating minor respiratory issues.

  • Scientific Name: Origanum vulgare
  • Benefits: Culinary use; antimicrobial and digestive aid
  • Cultivation: Full sun; trim regularly; well-drained soil
