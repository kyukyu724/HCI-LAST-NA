Cosmos Flower

Cosmos Flower is a brightly colored, daisy-like flower known for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation. In the Philippines, it is widely grown in gardens and public spaces to add vibrant color and attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. While mainly decorative, cosmos flowers also help support healthy garden ecosystems by encouraging beneficial insect activity.

  • Scientific Name: Cosmos bipinnatus
  • Benefits: Primarily ornamental; some suggest antioxidant properties.
  • Cultivation: Thrives in full sun with well-drained soil; drought-tolerant and attracts pollinators.
