Malunggay

Moringa commonly known as malunggay in the Philippines, is a fast-growing, drought-resistant tree celebrated for its high nutritional value. Filipinos often add its small, tender leaves to tinola (chicken soup) and other vegetable dishes to boost the meal's health benefits. It is also used medicinally for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

  • Scientific Name: Moringa oleifera
  • Benefits: Nutrient-dense leaves; anti-inflammatory.
  • Cultivation:Full sun; drought-tolerant; grow from seed or cutting.
