About Us

In August 2021, The Quezon City University - Center for Urban Agriculture and Innovation (QCU-CUAI) was established through the Board Resolution No. 14 s. 2021, with the aim of promoting urban agriculture as a measure to alleviate proverty, address the ability to supply, foster social integration among communities and protect the environment through eco-friendly methods and other alternative, and innovative gardening methods. United by a passion to advance knowledge, practices and innovations in the field of urban agriculture. The Quezon City Government through the Quezon City University Deaprtment of Agriculture entered a partnership laying the foundation of the center as a hub for research development, and training initiatives related to sustainable and innovative urban farming methods.

With the on-going efforts and commitment to Quezon City Government to achieve sustainable living for its Qcitizen, such as "Joy of Urban Farming.", the QCU-CUAI also commits itself to be recognized as the leader in urbang agriculture and innovation through the exploration and development of new technologies and approches thorugh research and extension. After through planning, training, and preparations, the QCU-CUAI begin its formal operation in June 2022, strategically focusing on four core functions: Research and Development, Training, Production and Business Development, and Partnerships and collaborations. QCU-CUAI aims to become a recognized hub for sustainable urban agriculture, fostering a network for urban farmers, researchers, academicians, innovators, and advocates dedicated to creating a more resilient and sustainable future for Quezon City and Beyone.
