Sunflower

Sunflower is a tall, vibrant plant grown both for its ornamental beauty and its seeds. In the Philippines, sunflowers are planted in gardens and farms for aesthetic purposes and, occasionally, for harvesting seeds that are roasted and eaten as snacks.

  • Scientific Name: Helianthus annuus
  • Benefits: Seeds for oil and snacks; pollinator attractor
  • Cultivation: Direct sow; full sun; harvest when heads brown
