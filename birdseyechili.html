Bird's Eye Chili

Bird's Eye Chili locally known as siling labuyo, is a small, fiery chili pepper. It's a common ingredient in Filipino cuisine, adding heat to dishes like Bicol Express and spicy condiments. The chili is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

  • Scientific Name: Capsicum frutescens
  • Benefits: High in capsaicin; boosts metabolism.
  • Cultivation: Full sun; regular watering; harvest when red.
